NTU Computational Astrophysics Lab

High-Performance Simulations of the Universe

About Us

The Computational Astrophysics Lab (CALab) uses large-scale numerical simulations to explore the physics of the Universe.

Our research directions:

1. Investigating the distinctive wave nature of fuzzy dark matter
2. Developing GAMER, a multi-GPU-accelerated AMR code for astrophysical simulations
3. Conducting collaborative, GAMER-based projects spanning galaxy clusters, dwarf galaxies, core-collapse supernovae, and star formation

See Research and Publications for details.
